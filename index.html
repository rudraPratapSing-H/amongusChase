🚨 Vent Chase

How to Play

You are the blue character. Your goal is to outsmart and defeat the red impostor before it escapes.

Controls

  • Desktop: Use Arrow Keys or WASD keys to move.
  • Mobile: Use the on-screen arrow buttons that appear once the game starts.

Rules & Objectives

Your Mission ✅

  • Complete Tasks (T): Move onto the green 'T' tiles.
  • Seal Vents (V): Move onto the cyan 'V' tiles to block the impostor's escape routes.

The Impostor 🔴

  • It hunts down tasks ('T') to sabotage them.
  • It gets scared and runs to a vent ('V') if you get too close.
  • It uses vents to teleport around the map.

How to Win 🏆

  1. Catch the Impostor by moving onto its tile.
  2. Trap the Impostor by completing all tasks AND sealing all vents.

🚨 Vent Chase

How to Lose ☠️

You lose if the impostor escapes through a vent after all tasks are completed.

Note: You must win the game to unlock the Form.
