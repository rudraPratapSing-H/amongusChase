🚨 Vent Chase

How to Play

You are the blue character. Your goal is to outsmart and defeat the red impostor before it escapes.

Controls

  • Tap / Click: The easiest way to play! Simply tap (on mobile) or click (on desktop) any open tile, and your character will automatically move there.
  • Keyboard: For precise, step-by-step movement, you can also use the Arrow Keys or WASD on a desktop computer.

Rules & Objectives

Your Mission ✅

  • Complete Tasks (T): When you pass through a green task tile ('T'), you complete it.
  • Seal Vents (V): When you pass through a cyan vent tile ('V'), you seal it.

The Impostor 🔴

  • When it passes through a task ('T'), it destroys it.
  • When it passes through a vent ('V'), it exits from another random vent.
  • It gets scared and runs to a vent if you get too close.

How to Win 🏆

  1. Catch the Impostor by moving onto its tile.(sometimes the impostor dodges)
  2. Trap the Impostor by completing all tasks AND sealing all vents.

How to Lose ☠️

You lose if the impostor escapes through a vent after all tasks are completed.

Note: You must win the game to unlock the Form.
