Vent Chase

How to Play

You (🟡) must chase and stop the Saboteur (🔴) before they escape!

How the Game Works

  • Click anywhere on the map to move to that spot.
  • The Saboteur moves randomly and tries to run away from you.
  • There are tasks (T) on the map. The Saboteur sabotages them by stepping on them.
  • Fix sabotaged tasks by moving over them.
  • There are vents (V). The Saboteur can teleport between vents and escape through one if you don’t stop them.
  • Tip: Move over a vent to seal it. Once sealed, the Saboteur cannot use that vent, making escape harder.

Win

  • Catch the Saboteur.
  • Or seal/block all vents before they escape.

Lose

  • If the Saboteur sabotages all tasks and escapes through a vent.

Controls

🖱️ Click/tap a tile to move.
⌨️ Use W, A, S, D or arrow keys.
