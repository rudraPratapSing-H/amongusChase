Vent Chase

You (🔵) must stop the Saboteur (🔴) from escaping!

  • Move over green tasks (T) to keep systems online.
  • Fix red tasks sabotaged by the Saboteur.
  • Seal cyan vents (V) to block escape routes.

Win: Catch the Saboteur or seal all vents.
Lose: If all tasks are sabotaged and the Saboteur escapes.

Controls

🖱️ Tap a tile to move.
⌨️ Use WASD or arrow keys.
